I AM STRUGGLING WITH COUNCIL TAX AND OTHER UNSECURE DEBTS, WHAT CAN I DO? +

If you are struggling to make payments, do not panic. We will do everything we can to find a suitable solution. As soon as you find yourself struggling, please get in touch with us to see if we can help. You are not alone!

WHAT WILL HAPPEN TO THE INTEREST AND CHARGES ON MY DEBTS? +

Depending on your chosen solution, we may be able to freeze the interest and stop the bank charges.

HOW LONG DOES THE PROCESS TAKE?+

We can get to work on stopping any unreasonable creditors right away. Our debt experts will explain all options available so you can make an informed decision.

WHAT WILL MY MONTHLY PAYMENTS BE?+

The monthly payments are usually as much as you can reasonably afford after you have accounted for living costs and dependents if you have them. The amount should be the most you can afford and that is achievable.
